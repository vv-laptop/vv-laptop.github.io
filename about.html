About Rani

What’s the Difference that Makes the Difference ?”

“ I am passionate about helping leaders raise their game using important truths and practical approaches which have emerged working with hundreds of top leaders across the world and the latest scientific evidence on how to harness human potential. “The difference that makes the difference” are the insights about self and how to engage the world to succeed in their lives and on their terms. Only a small proportion of the population (3-5%) are able to really leverage their inherent power in this way. Considerable advances in our understanding of how to optimise human functioning and well-being allows us now to establish the inner roadmap and practical habits which are the foundational basis for outer success”

Founder of One-Zero Leadership, Rani Bains.

My experience as a business psychologist, leadership consultant and executive coach spans 3 decades in leading edge boutique consultancies focused on helping to assess and develop senior leaders and executives in FTSE 100 organisations across all sectors and all levels of the organisations, within the private and public arenas. I have been able to work with hundreds of leaders in global organisations to understand the internal and external factors which can enable or support success as well as factors likely to impede progress and well- being. My expertise in profiling has given me depth of insight and well considered nuanced judgement into the characteristics and judgment needed to support critical recruitment decisions, identify top performers and accelerate high potential and leadership development through deploying evidence-based approaches..



My area of expertise lies in deploying the new and emerging fields of positive psychology and the neuroscience of optimal performance to maximise workforce engagement, and determine performance outcomes. I have lectured and authored numerous lectures and texts on “The Art and Science of Human Flourishing” drawing on deep experience and curiosity to establish the insights and habits needed to move to next level where people have more resilience/energy, establish relationships, inspire with presence, can innovate and engage in meaningful way to deliver value to themselves their communities and society.



Some career highlights

I have a Masters in Clinical Psychology and am an ICF accredited coach. I set up one of the first EAP services in the U.K. and as the Clinical Director, was responsible for 200 members of the coaching faculty. I also co- wrote the Ethical Guidelines for the EAP profession. 25 years of consultancy experience as a leadership advisor for senior executives in the 50% of the FTSE companies. Faculty Member for the HR Guild. Designed and delivered their Future Leaders Programme. One of the authors of Meaning Inc - A Blueprint for Success in the 21st Century. Invited to write a peer reviewed paper and lecture at the World Humanities Conference, UNESCO on “The Art and Science of Human Flourishing”.
